Explore the Groups of The Fellowship Centre

Youth Groups

Fellowship Kids
The Fellowship Kids Church is for ages birth through grade 5.

It’s on YouTube every Sunday at 10:30am. Our online program is fully programmed for your kids which includes Bible stories, activities and loads of fun! We exist for families. Our heart is that the Fellowship Kids Ministry would serve to be an effective resource for parents leading their families to follow Jesus.

Fellowship Youth
It’s about crafting an atmosphere where they can belong. We encourage the Youth of The Fellowship Centre Church to be involved in everything we do from inviting, participating in an online campus and serving on our online ministry. These nights are characterized by engaging activities, praise and worship, biblical teaching, and small group discipleship for young people grade six to grade twelve. As a part of small group discipleship, Fellowship Youth also hosts social activities on a monthly basis.
