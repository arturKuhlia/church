Who Are We?

The Fellowship Centre Church is a new church plant in the Durham Area.
The Senior Pastor, Vira Stewart of the True Church of Jesus Christ Fellowship in Scarborough, Ontario, has embarked on a vision to expand the gospel to the City of Oshawa. The vision was to start a new church plant and spread the good news under the direction of the Holy Spirit. Asst. Pastor Jermaine & Evg. Deneen Johnson were assigned to this ministry and have worked alongside Senior Pastor Vira Stewart. July 1, 2020 Sr. Pastor Vira Stewart once again saw it fit and time to hand over the leadership of this ministry to Pastors Jermaine & Deneen Johnson to continue providing the services needed to maintain the ministry in Durham, Ontario.  

Meet the People Behind TCJC

Elder Jermaine Johnson is the Pastor of a new church plant in the city of Oshawa, Ontario with his wife and their four beautiful children. He is dedicated to living by example and encouraging men and women how to live up to their full potential in Christ. Elder Johnson has more than a decade of teaching and sharing the word, which enables him to reach others with warmth, transparency and strength. He is a husband, father, teacher, conference speaker, role model and mentor.
Elder Johnson was born in Toronto, Canada and became a member of the True Church of Jesus Christ Fellowship Centre in 1997. Elder Johnson assisted the growth of many ministries such as the Christian Education, Community Outreach Department, Men’s group Leader and Church retreats. With his start as a Sunday School Teacher, he has become an eloquent Bible Teacher and a dynamic preacher of the gospel of Jesus Christ.
Elder Johnson serves his Church community assiduously with a great passion for the kingdom of God. His infamous past in the streets of Toronto has become the impetus behind his passion to evangelize souls all across the city of Toronto and the GTA. His mission is to restore, prepare and preserve souls for the Kingdom of God. He is not only an example to many; but he is also a Mentor and Counsellor to many souls languished by the wiles of the devil. He is a man with an inveterate desire to pursue the mind of Christ and also lead others to fulfill their destiny in GOD.

Evangelist Deneen Johnson is the wife of Pastor, Elder Jermaine Johnson. Together they have four beautiful girls; Mackenzie, Samantha, Lauren, and Jennifer. They started a new church plant in the city of Oshawa, Ontario. She is dedicated to living by example and encouraging women and men how to live up to their full potential in Christ. Evangelist Johnson has more than a decade of directing the choir, mentoring youth as Youth President, motivating women as Women’s leader and as Founder of Couple’s Ministry Building better marriages is the key. Sharing the word enables her to reach others with warmth, transparency and strength. She is a wife, mother, teacher, conference speaker, role model and mentor.

Evangelist Johnson serves her Church community tirelessly with a great passion for the kingdom of God. Her mission is to help mentor women, to restore, prepare and preserve souls for the Kingdom of God. She is not only an example to many; but he is also a Leader and Counselor to many souls languished by the wiles of the devil. She is a woman with an desire to pursue the mind of Christ and also lead others to fulfill their destiny in GOD.
