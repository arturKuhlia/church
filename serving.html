Find Ways to Serve

The Fellowship Centre Church believes you are created to make a difference. Serving with The Fellowship Centre Church can happen in many ways. When you join us in serving, you will not only bless others, but you will also make friends and contribute to our common purpose in God’s global mission.

Here's How You Can Help

  • Fellowship Production
    Fellowship Production ministry is looking for audio & visual volunteers passionate about worship to join our team. Training will be provided.
  • Fellowship Compassion
    Hope Compassion consists of several opportunities to demonstrate Christ’s love to our community in practical ways.
  • Fellowship Welcome Team
    The Fellowship Welcome Team is looking for welcoming and engaging people to serve as greeters, ushers, tellers, in hospitality and at our Guest Reception.
  • Fellowship Worship
    Hope Worship is looking for musicians and vocalists to join our team. Auditions are held throughout the year.
  • Fellowship Kids
    Fellowship Kids is looking for volunteers grade 6 and up who have a passion to see children’s loves transformed with the gospel.
  • Fellowship Facility & Setup
    Our Facility & Setup team is looking for volunteers to help with setup, tear down, and general facility maintenance.
  • Fellowship Admin & Office Support
    Our church office is looking for help to carry out administrative tasks during the week. No experience necessary.
  • Fellowship Prayer Team
    If you have a passion for prayer, we would love to hear from you.
