Donations by E-Transfer

E-Transfer is a simple, convenient, and secure way to send money directly from one bank account to another. All you need is access to online or mobile banking through your financial institution, and you can send your donation directly to The Fellowship Centre Church via e-transfer.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR DONATIONS BY E-TRANSFER*
1. Add The Fellowship Centre Church as a new e-transfer recipient with the email address thefellowshipcentredurham@gmail.com
2. Skip setting a security question and no password is needed as Hope Bible Church Markham has Auto Deposit set up.
3. IMPORTANT: Please be sure to include your Name and Address in the Message/Note section of the transaction (we need this information in order to issue a tax receipt for this donation). We realized some online bank services limit comments to only 40 characters, if this is the case with your bank and you’re unable to enter your entire name and address then please also send a separate e-mail with your name, address, and the amount of the donation separately to thefellowshipcentredurham@gmail.com

*NOTE: Unless otherwise stated, donations will be designated to the General Fund. If you’d like your donation designated to something other than the General Fund please indicate in the Message/Note section what you are wanting your donation to go to (ie. Building Fund, Special Offering, etc.) 

Online/Mobile Bank Payments  

If you bank with Scotiabank, Royal Bank or Bank of Montreal, you can now give to the church by simply visiting the bank’s website or mobile app and setting up “The Fellowship Centre Church” as a payee. This allows you to make donations to the church in the same manner as you pay your other bills online. If you’d like to use this giving method, you will need to set up an account number with the church so that we can receipt/track your donation. To obtain your own account number or for further details, please email thefellowshipcentredurham@gmail.com 

PayPal

Just go to the section below and you can make a one-time gift by using the “Donate with PayPal” under “General Donation”. You can also choose to set up regular recurring giving through PayPal. These will be charged to your credit card.

Mail or Drop Off

You can mail your offering to the church. (please don’t leave cash in the mailbox).
