You Belong Here

We want to take a moment to extend a very warm welcome to everyone who's visiting us for the first time this morning. Whether you're just having a look, or are searching out for a place to worship, we're delighted to have you here.

Get a Glimpse of Our Service:

Welcome to The Fellowship Centre

We're all about Jesus Christ. We believe that Jesus Christ was who he said he was, the Son of God who came and died for our sins. So if this is your first time in church, we hope that get to know Him as we do, because He's truly what life is all about for us!

We’re all about people. Every person who walks through our doors is important to us, so if you ever feel like just another number, we're doing something wrong and I give you permission to tell me about it.

As a group of God's people we care about you and your family and we're here not only to feed you. We’re all about relationships. We hope to cultivate and grow meaningful and positive relationships between another, but also go further by getting and growing in relationship with God. That's us in a nutshell!

I want to encourage you to fill out one of our connect cards that you'll find in the bulletin you got when you came in. Just write your name and e-mail or phone number on there, since we'd love to stay in touch with you and let you know about some exciting upcoming events we're planning for the next few months.  

Spiritually, but to help in any way we can. We love doing it and it's why we exist!
Also, if you've got a specific prayer request, please take the courage to write it down on that same piece of paper (there's a space for it), and put it in the prayer box at the back of this room when you leave.

We'd love to pray for you, but if you don't ask us, there's nothing for us to pray for, so I encourage you to make use of this opportunity and allow us to serve you in this way.

Enjoy the rest of your worship time with us, and God bless you! 

Vision

The Fellowship Centre Church is ministering to the hurting in full dependence on the Word of God, the Spirit of God, and the community of God to experience transforming life change.

Serving

At The Fellowship Centre we believe you were created to make a difference. Serving with The Fellowship Centre Durham can happen in many ways. When you join us in serving, you will not only bless others, but you will also make friends and contribute to our common purpose in God’s global mission.

Groups

At The Fellowship Centre we know that we are better together. We know that it can be difficult to know how to connect with others. Joining our various Groups, such as Men’s, Women’s, Youth, Couples, and Single etc. will be a life changing experience where you can make a connection and build bridges. We also believe you will be a part of a vibrant community where you can connect with God.

Fellowship Counselling 

If you are facing a particular situation or struggle in your life that seems difficult to cope with or overcome, we want to come alongside you to help. We hope to lovingly care for you and help carry your burdens (Galatians 6:1-2; James 1:2-5) but, more importantly, point you to and remind you of the ultimate hope, Jesus Christ. We would love to sit down and talk with you about how we can help.
